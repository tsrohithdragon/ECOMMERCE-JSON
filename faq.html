HELP WITH YOUR ORDER

Do i need to open an account in shop with you ?

If you use PayPal for payments you will need a bank account to connect to it to withdraw profits or pay bills you can't pay from within PayPal. I suggest you check with PayPal for their latest terms in your country but I don't think they insist on a bank account for a business and will be happy with a personal account (perfect for self employed people). If you want to offer payment for customers using card clearance (other than PayPal's card clearance service or similar services) I would expect you will need to get a business bank account to get this sorted. However I would recommend not doing that as you are just starting so you can keep your costs low. Good luck..
